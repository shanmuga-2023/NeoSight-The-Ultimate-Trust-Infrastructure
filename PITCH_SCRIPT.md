# Hackathon Pitch Script: NeoSight 🎙️

*Target Runtime: ~4 minutes*

## 🚀 Opening Hook (10–15 sec)
Every day, forged documents slip through systems that are supposed to protect us—fake IDs, manipulated invoices, AI-generated credentials.

The real problem isn’t just detection.
It’s **trust**.

Because today’s systems say:
> “This looks fake… but we can’t tell you why.”

And in the real world—**that’s useless.**

---

## 💡 Problem (20 sec)
Current verification tools are:
* Black-box AI models
* Outputting vague probability scores
* Offering zero explainability

Which means:
* Governments can’t rely on them
* Recruiters can’t defend decisions
* Border control can’t justify rejections

👉 If you can’t explain a decision, you can’t **trust** it.

---

## 🔍 Solution (30 sec)
We built **NeoSight** — a next-generation **trust infrastructure for document verification**.

Not just detecting forgery…
But:
✔ Explaining it
✔ Proving it
✔ Making it actionable

---

## ⚙️ How It Works (40 sec)
NeoSight runs a **Tri-Factor Forensic Engine**:

1. **Visual Layer**
   * Detects tampering using Error Level Analysis
   * Highlights suspicious regions with heatmaps
2. **Structural Layer**
   * Identifies typography inconsistencies like kerning anomalies
   * Detects layout mismatches
3. **Metadata Layer**
   * Flags hidden inconsistencies and missing signatures

---

## 🧠 The Breakthrough (30 sec)
What makes NeoSight different is **Explainable AI**:

Instead of:
> “Forgery detected: 82%”

We say:
> “Signature region shows abnormal compression inconsistent with the rest of the document.”

👉 We turn AI into **forensic evidence**, not just predictions.

---

## 🔐 Trust Layer (20 sec)
We also simulate a **blockchain-backed verification system**:
* Documents are hashed
* Compared against trusted templates
* Any mismatch breaks the integrity chain

---

## 🧪 Demo Flow (30 sec)
In our live demo:
1. Upload a document
2. Watch NeoSight scan it in real time
3. See heatmaps highlight tampered regions
4. Get a clear forensic explanation
5. View the final trust verdict

Anywhere **trust matters**.
